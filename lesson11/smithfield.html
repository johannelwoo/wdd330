Explore the City

  • Travel the Oregon Trail to see 14 historic sites, including the Sheep Rock Point, Wagon Box Grave, Carbonated Springs and the World's Only Captive Geyser.
  • Visit Chesterfield which is near the National Historic District. Here you can learn about the historic trains, dams, and the farming and mining that built this city.
  • Visit Formation Springs and Cave Preserve to view wildlife.
  • Other activities you can do in our town include golf, skiing, ice skating and snowmobiling!
