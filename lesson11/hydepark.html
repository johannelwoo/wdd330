Explore the City

  • Come joing the community for an Easter Egg Scramble on March 31 starting at 10am at the Preston City Park.
  • Come enjoy the warm water at the Oneida Narrows Reservoir where you can float the narrows or travel a little farther up the road to the reservoir and go boating, fishing or swimming.
  • Take a relaxing trip to Maple Grove Hot Springs which provides a natural hot spring therapy pools, gift ship and even camping by the Oneida Narrows Reservoir.
  • In the winter we have the perfect snow to enjoy snowmobiling and cross county skiing.
  • If you love golf, come to our 18-hole course which is open to the public everyday with affordable rates.
