Drought = Fire hazard

Remember as the 24th of July now approaches that we have been asked to not do any fireworks this year to decrease the chances of accidentally starting a fire. It has been a really dry year and county and state officials are worried about doing fire works during this terrible drought.

Time to conserve!

The governor of Utah has extended our state of emergency for a drought. Do whatever you can to help conserve water. One thing you can do is to try to water your lawn in the morning or at night. Also limit your waterings to one or twice a week if you are not under restrictions.
